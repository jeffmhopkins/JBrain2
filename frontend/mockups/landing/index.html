Five takes on the same idea, tuned calm for phone: a slow flock of 10 boids drifting through the top two-thirds, a lone predator among them, and a live neural net pinned to the bottom third. Tap a boid — it changes color and the neurons below light up with that boid's state. No menus, no chrome. All five share one dependency-free, pure-canvas software-3D engine; they differ only in feel.
