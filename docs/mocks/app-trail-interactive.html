What this mock shows

  • Color metric toggle — recolor the whole trail by Speed (default), Accel, or Battery. The legend (top-left, under the names) updates to that metric's scale.
  • Tap a point on the trail — a map callout (“J: 35 mph”, in the active metric) pins to that point, and the card below fills in everything: time, speed, heading vector, accel, battery, etc.
  • Span buttons set the slider range — 1h / 8h / 1d / 7d choose the total span; the dual start/stop slider scrubs a sub-window within it.
  • Drag an end of the slider — if the data point at that end was moving, the card shows that point's readout live as you scrub (and the callout follows it).

Nothing here is wired to the backend — it's a synthetic trip so the interactions are real to click through.
