Priya Nair is a pediatrician and the founder of Nair Pediatrics in Brookline.[9] She is the younger sister of Jordan Hale.[2]

Early life

Nair grew up in Austin, Texas, where she was known within the family as a science enthusiast from an early age.[1] In 2019 she was admitted to medical school at Johns Hopkins.[2]

Career

Nair is a pediatrician who trained at a children's clinic before founding her own practice.

Training and early career

After completing her residency in 2022, Nair worked as a pediatrician at Riverside Children's Clinic.[4]

Nair Pediatrics

In 2024 she left Riverside to open her own practice, Nair Pediatrics, in Brookline, where she currently practices.[9]

Talks and publications

  • Co-authored a paper on vaccine hesitancy in JAMA Pediatrics (2023).[16]
  • Presented at the regional conference on childhood nutrition (2024).[17]
  • Gave a talk at the state pediatric conference on childhood asthma (2025).[13]

Personal life

Nair married Tom in a small courthouse ceremony in 2021, attended only by family.[3] Their first daughter, Anaya, was born in 2024,[7] and their second, Mira, in 2026.[20] In 2025 the family moved to a larger home in Brookline.[12]
